About Us

Supporting Men's Mental Health in Kensington & Chelsea, Hammersmith & Fulham

MENd is a community interest company dedicated to men's mental health through walk and talk sessions. In the UK, 12.5% of men have a mental health disorder, with suicide rates at 17.4 per 100,000 for males—three times higher than women. Half of men have experienced poor mental health, yet 40% never talk about it. Our sessions harness the benefits of talking, which reduces loneliness, improves focus, and builds coping strategies, combined with the mood-enhancing effects of walking in nature.
